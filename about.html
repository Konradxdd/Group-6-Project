About Us

Welcome to Polish Air, your one-stop solution for hassle-free flight booking. We connect travelers with the best deals worldwide, ensuring a smooth and secure booking experience. Whether you're planning a business trip, a dream vacation, or a last minute getaway, we help travellers to find the best flight deals worldwide with ease and confidence.

Our Story

Polish Air started as a university group project in 2025, with a simple goal to make air travel more accessible, stress-free, and affordable. As a team of passionate travelers and tech enthusiasts, we realized that booking flights was often complicated, with fluctuating prices and hidden fees.

We built a platform that combines cutting-edge technology with a user-friendly interface, ensuring that everyone can find the best airfare deals within seconds. What started as a classroom idea has now grown into a global service, helping thousands of travelers book flights with confidence.

Our Mission

We aim to make air travel simple, affordable, and accessible by providing a seamless booking experience and excellent customer support.

Rules and regulations for Air Ticket Booking

Booking confirmation: All bookings will confirm via email or sms.
Check-in time: Passangers must check-in at least 2 hours flights.
Baggage Allowance: Each passangers is allowed a specified amount of luggage. Extra baggage may apply.
Refund policy: Refunds are processed with in 7-14 days of booking date based on fare rules.
Identification requirement: All passangers must carry a valid government issued ID for verifications.
Flight schedule changes: Airlines reserve the right to reschedule or cancel flights with prior notice.
Cancellation policy: Tickets can be cancelled up to 24 hours before departure with applicable charges.
